Recipe #1 可乐排骨

  • Time: 60 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
排骨 500克
可乐 200克
姜片，葱 各40克
八角，老抽，盐 各10克

Step 1: 将排骨洗净切寸状，然后锅中烧水，烧开后放入排骨，等水即将再次滚开时将排骨捞出，用凉水冲洗干净、滤干水分备用(过水的目的是为了撇去排骨中的杂质，让口感更鲜美)
Step 2:用花生油爆香姜片(3片)，再加入焯好的排骨爆炒
Step 3:然后将炒好的排骨放入瓦煲，再放少量八角和老抽、盐、可乐
Step 4:用中火煲至收汁就好，上台时放少量的葱段

Recipe #2 Thai Steamed Salmon

  • Time: 90 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Salmon 300g
Garlic 4 cloves
Coriander 1 bunch
Fish sauce, lime juice 20 ml each

Step 1: Marinate the salmon with grounded garlic and coriander with fish sauce and lime juice for at least an hour
Step 2:Put the marinated salmon into a steamer to steam for 20 minutes
Step 3:Serve with a squeeze of lime and a pinch of coriander

Recipe #3 Clam chowder

  • Time: 90 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Clams 500g
Bacon 50g
Onions 110g
Potatoes 225g

Step 1: Clean the clams and discard any which do not close when tapped.
Step 2:Heat a large lidded frying pan until hot, add the clams and the water and bring to the boil. Cover with a lid and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until all the clams have opened - discard any which remain closed.
Step 3:Strain the clams, reserving the cooking liquour. Remove all the clams from their shells and set aside, discarding the shells.
